TAQ / System Admin

  • MFR intake & post job positions to vendors
  • Route vendor-shortlisted profiles to managers
  • Monitor end-to-end hiring pipeline
  • Super admin: users, roles, permissions
  • Does NOT schedule interviews, onboard, or run BGV

HR Operations

  • Schedule interviews after manager selection
  • Send offers & contractor onboarding
  • BGV, documents, assignments
  • Register/activate vendors & compliance
  • Own timesheet process — approve after supervisor
  • Validate invoices against SOW terms

Finance

  • Rate card & billing history
  • Process approved invoices → payment batches
  • Timesheet review = read-only final check
  • Dual approval > $10,000
  • Quarterly spend reports

Contractor's Manager

  • Raise MFR to TAQ
  • Review profiles forwarded by TAQ
  • Select candidates needed for role
  • Supervisor: first approval on contractor timesheets
  • Performance ratings & SOW feedback

Vendor Side Manager

  • Respond to TA job orders (Step 16)
  • Submit candidate profiles
  • Onboarding & offboarding coordination
  • SOW compliance & deliverables
  • Approve finance payments (Step 18)
  • Track end dates & performance

Contractor

  • Submit documents to HR
  • Cooperate with HR on interviews
  • Submit own weekly timesheets (start of approval chain)
  • Upload documents via portal
  • Request leave (with balance shown)

End-to-End Journeys

  1. Hiring Orchestration: Manager MFR → TAQ posts to vendor → Vendor shortlists → TAQ routes to manager → Manager selects → HR schedules interview → HR offer & onboarding → Finance rate → Timesheet → Vendor payment approval
  2. Timesheet to Payment: Contractor Submits → Supervisor Approves → HR Ops Approves → Finance Final Check (pay rates, dates, anomalies) → Clear for Vendor Pay / Block → Finance Batch → Vendor Pay
  3. Invoice Management (recurring): Vendor Raises → HR Ops validates vs SOW → TA Approves → Finance Processes → Paid / Disputed
  4. Assignment Transfer: Initiate Transfer → Select New Project/Manager → Approval → Close Old Assignment → Open New → Notify Stakeholders → Clear Anomaly
  5. Contractor Exit: End Date Trigger → Exit Checklist → Access Revocation → Final Timesheet → Invoice Closure → Record Archive → Stakeholder Notifications
